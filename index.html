Prediction Game

In this game, you are a chipmunk digging around underground in search of food.

You need to be able to get to the right place at the right time in order to eat.

In the 1st round, you need to predict where the food will go before it moves.
In the 2nd round, you need to predict when the food will move to a certain location.

Before we begin, please read the instructions on how to play the game.
  • 1. IMPORTANT: Make sure you are using Google Chrome on fullscreen in INCOGNITO mode.
  • 2. IMPORTANT: Enable Popups! Click (in the top right corner of your browser) then click "Always allow pop-ups from http://jwzhang.com". If you don't see that icon, click here.
  • 2. Close all other open applications. The game may lag if you don't do this.
  • 3. Make sure you get to the end screen that will say "You are finished!" on it.
  • 4. Use the arrow keys to select where you think the food will go. Make sure you click PRIOR to the food moving.
  • 5. After you click start, the game will start immediately. Pay close attention!
  • 6. Try your best and have fun!
